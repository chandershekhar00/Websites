Checklist
Documents Required
Keep these documents ready before you begin your application.

🆔 Identity & Address Proof

  • Aadhaar Card (required for OTP auth)
  • Voter ID / PAN Card
  • Driving License
  • Utility bill (not older than 3 months)
  • Bank Statement with address

📸 Photographs

  • 2 recent passport-size photographs (white background)
  • Photo size: 35mm × 45mm
  • Face should cover 70–80% of the frame
  • No spectacles, no headgear (exceptions for religious reasons)

📅 Date of Birth Proof

  • Birth Certificate (issued by Municipal Authority)
  • School Transfer Certificate with DOB
  • Matriculation Certificate
  • Aadhaar Card showing DOB

📋 For Renewals

  • Original old passport (if available)
  • Self-attested copy of first and last page
  • Self-attested copy of visa page (if applicable)
  • Address proof if address changed
